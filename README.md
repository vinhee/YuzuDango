# Milestone 1 Submission

Team Name: YuzuDango

Proposed Level of Achievement: Gemini

## Motivation

After stressful exam or assignment submission periods, we enjoy playing games together to de-stress. Therefore, we'd like to challenge ourselves to recreate the cozy vibe our favourite games have given us (e.g. Stardew Valley, Minecraft). In the process, we hope to explore aspects of game design and improve our programming skills.

## Vision

Our game "My Little Shop" will be a pixel art style cozy game with a farming-collection-crafting-selling loop.

## Storyline

### Setting

My Little Shop starts when the MC inherits an abandoned store in the woods and is determined to transform that rundown building into the shop of their dreams.

## User Stories

As a player, I want to play a game with cozy vibes.

## Scope of Project

My Little Shop is a single-player PC farming sim game set in a plot of land in a forest, with pixel art style, where players explore and uncover new ingredients and crafting recipes.

Players can plant crops or forage them around the map, gathering resources to craft items at their respective workstations. More crafting recipes are unlocked upon discovering new ingredients and earning money.

My Little Shop’s features are outlined in the following sections, organised by the following tags:

- [Proposed] - features for Minimum Viable Product (MVP) by Splashdown

- [Additional Features] - add-on features to improve product after MVP is completed

## Map Exploration

### [Proposed]

The map will have the following different biomes:

| Biome | Elements | Purpose |
|---------|---------|---------|
| Farmlands | Store, grass, rocks, trees | The main storefront will be located here, with tillable ground to allow players to grow crops. |
| Forest | Trees, forageable objects such as mushrooms | Introduce foraging so players have the option to collect some crops without farming. |
| Beach | Sand and forageable objects such as seashells, seaweed. Sea water and fishes. | Introduce more ingredients that users can discover. |

### [Current Progress]

Currently, our map design has not been finalised, but we are able to implement the grassy areas and forageable objects.

### [Additional Features]

Different biomes can be added to make the map more interactive and the experience more immersive:

| Additional Biome | Elements | Purpose |
|------------------|----------|----------|
| Quarry / Rocky Area | Stones, different types of ores with different durability and rarity | Increase depth to the game by providing multiple tiers of ores and equipment (Iron, Gold, Diamond, etc.) |
| Flower Area | Different types of flowers that have various uses | Increase depth to the game as players are able to unlock new recipes and craft more products to sell |
